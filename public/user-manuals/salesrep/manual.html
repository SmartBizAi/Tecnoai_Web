Section 2
Clock In / Clock Out

As a Sales Rep, you must clock in when you start your shift and clock out when you finish. This records your working hours for payroll calculation.

Currently Working
02:34:17
 

How to Clock In

  • Look for the ▶ Clock In button in the top header bar (visible at the top of every page).
  • Tap it at the start of your shift. The button changes to a green timer showing your elapsed time.

How to Clock Out

  • When your shift ends, tap the ⏹ Clock Out button in the header.
  • Your time entry is automatically saved and sent to your admin for review and payment.
⚠️
Don't forget to clock out! If you forget, your entry shows as "In Progress" and your admin will need to manually correct the end time. Unclosed shifts can affect your pay calculation.

Section 4
Creating a New Booking

Creating a booking is your most important daily task. Follow these steps every time a customer wants to reserve a service.

📸
Screenshot: Fleet Order Modal — Category Selection
Shows the service category grid: Jet Ski, ATV, Yacht, and any custom categories

Step 1 — Choose the Service Type

A grid of service buttons appears. Tap the one the customer wants:

OptionDescription
🚤 Jet SkisPersonal watercraft rentals — single or double rider options
🚜 ATV / QuadAll-terrain vehicle experiences on land
🛥️ YachtsYacht charters and boat experiences
Custom categoriesAny additional services your company offers (e.g., Parasailing 🪂)

Step 2 — Build the Order

This section lets you add one or more activities to the booking cart:

  • Activity: Select from the dropdown the specific tour or experience (filtered by category).
  • Target Area: Type the customer's preferred area (e.g., "Miami Beach"). This is optional but helps find nearby ports.
  • Port/Location: Select from the list of suggested nearby ports. The list shows the port name, address, and distance ordered by proximity.
  • Quantity:
    • For Jet Skis: Enter number of Single riders 👤 and/or Double riders 👥 separately.
    • For ATV / Yacht: Enter the total number of Units.
  • Duration: Select how long the activity lasts: 1 hour, 2 hours, 3 hours, 4 hours, 6 hours, or 8 hours.
  • Price: The system automatically fills the price from your configured pricing rules. If no rule exists, a badge says "No rule — enter manually" — type the price in the field.
  • Click Add to Order to add this activity. The item appears in the cart below. You can add multiple activities for the same group.
📸
Screenshot: Fleet Order — Activity Builder & Cart
Shows the activity dropdown, port selector, quantity inputs, and the cart with added items
💡
Multi-Activity Orders: If a group wants Jet Skiing AND ATVs, add both! Each activity is a separate cart item with its own price. The system calculates the combined total automatically.

Step 3 — Customer Information

Every booking needs a name and phone number:

Step 5 — Promo Code & Referral

If the customer has a promo code, enter it in the field and tap Apply. If valid, the discount is subtracted from the total automatically.

Instagram · TikTok · Google · Facebook · Friend/Referral · Flyer · Walk-in · Other

Step 7 — Confirm the Order

  • Click Confirm Order (or Confirm & Send Link if card payment was selected).
  • The booking is created and a success screen appears.
  • Choose an SMS template to send a confirmation text to the customer (recommended!).
  • Optionally choose an Email template as well.
  • Preview the message — the customer's name, date, time, and location are automatically inserted.
  • Click Send & Finish to send and close the modal.
💡
Save a Draft: If you get interrupted mid-booking, click Save Draft at the bottom. Your work is saved for 24 hours. Come back and click the draft dropdown at the top of the modal to restore it.

Availability Check

While filling out the booking form, the system automatically checks if the selected location has capacity for the chosen date and time. Look for:

  • 🟢 Green message — Location is available! Shows available unit count.
  • 🔴 Red message — Location is fully booked or closed for that time slot.

Managing Existing Bookings

Go to the Bookings page in the left sidebar to see all reservations. From here you can find any booking, check its status, and take action on it.

📸
Screenshot: Bookings Page — List View

Finding a Booking

Use the filter bar at the top to narrow down the list:

FilterUse it when...
← Today →Navigate day-by-day to see bookings for a specific date
Customer searchType the customer name or phone to find their booking instantly
Booking RefCustomer has a reference number and you need to pull it up fast
Status filterShow only CONFIRMED, PENDING, COMPLETED, etc.
LocationView bookings for a specific port only

Booking Status Meanings

StatusWhat It MeansWhat You Should Do
CONFIRMEDReservation confirmed, all goodNothing — just make sure they show up
PENDINGAwaiting confirmation or paymentFollow up with the customer
COMPLETEDActivity was done successfullyNo action needed
CANCELLEDBooking was cancelledCheck notes for reason
DRAFTNot fully submitted yetComplete and confirm the booking

What You Can Do on a Booking

Click any booking row to open its full detail view. As a Sales Rep, you can:

💳
Send Payment Link
Send a Stripe link to the customer for the remaining balance
📊
Update Status
Mark as Confirmed, Completed, or Cancelled
✏️
Edit Details
Correct quantities, notes, or customer info if needed
💬
Resend Message
Send another SMS or email confirmation to the customer
🚫
Note: Sales Reps cannot delete bookings or access admin-only features like Staff Management, Pricing, or Leads CRM. If you need to remove a booking, contact your admin.

Calendar View

Toggle to Calendar View using the button near the top of the Bookings page. This shows a full month grid with dots on days that have bookings — useful for spotting busy vs. slow days at a glance.

Section 6
Map View

The Map View shows all active fleet locations pinned on an interactive map. Use it to help customers find the nearest port or to look up a location's details before booking.

📸
Screenshot: Map View
Shows Google Map with colored pins for each port and the location detail panel

How to Use the Map

  • Use the category filter on the left to show only Jet Ski, ATV, or Yacht locations.
  • Enter a customer address + radius (in miles) to see which ports are within reach — the list sorts from closest to farthest.
  • Click a map pin to see the location's name, address, category, and status.
  • Click Book Now on any location card to open the booking modal with that location pre-filled — saves time!
⚠️
Credit Usage: Each map load and address search uses 1 credit. Avoid refreshing the map unnecessarily.
💳
Section 7
Credits

Credits power the platform. As a Sales Rep, you can view the current credit balance but cannot purchase credits (that's handled by your admin).

Seeing the Balance

Look at the left sidebar at the bottom — there's a credit balance pill. The color tells you the status:

ColorBalanceAction Needed
🟢 Gray/Green50+ creditsAll good — keep working
🟠 Orange10–50 creditsAlert your admin — low balance
🔴 Red (blinking)Below 10Urgent — notify admin immediately, bookings may fail
